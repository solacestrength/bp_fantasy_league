Rules and Scoring


  • Predict a winner for all weight classes.

  • Choose a confidence level for your predictions. 1 = least confident and 16 = most confident. Each correct guess is added to your points total - highest score wins!

  • You will have the option to predict the winning total of each weight class, which will be used to work out your accuracy score.
    This will be used as a tiebreaker if required; if two or more players finish at the top of the leaderboard with equal points, the player of those with the highest accuracy score wins.

  • Finally, you will predict a male and female best lifter - final tie-breaker.

  • Your responses will be emailed to you upon form completion. Please also check your spam/junk folders for these.

  • Entries close on Thursday 4th December 2025 at 11.59pm. You will be able to edit your responses in the entry form until this date. All the best!
