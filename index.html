📊 Rules and Scoring

  • Each entrant predicts a winner for all male and female weight classes (8 per gender).
  • Assign a confidence level for your predictions. 1 = least confident and 16 = most confident. Each correct guess is added to your points total - highest score wins! Placing more points on a prediction in a stacked weight class is high risk, high reward - choose wisely!
  • Entrants will also have the option to predict the winning total of each weight class. Don't forget your chips 🍟 (0.5kg multiples). This will be used as a tiebreaker if required. If two or more entrants finish at the top of the leaderboard, the entrant who most accurately predicted all 16 winning totals will win.
  • Finally, entrants will also predict a male and female winner for the best lifter award. This is will be used as a final tie-breaker if required.
  • Your responses will be emailed to you upon form completion. Please also check your spam/junk folders for these.
  • Entries close on Thursday 4th December 2025 at 11.59pm. You will be able to edit your responses in the Google form until this date. All the best!
